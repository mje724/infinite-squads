## Description

Build the squad history never saw coming.

Infinite Squads is a collectible card battler packed with 500 stylized legends, icons, oddballs, and agents of chaos. Open packs, chase rare pulls, discover wild chemistry combinations, and assemble a lineup that can survive increasingly absurd battle scenarios.

FEATURES

• Collect a roster of 500 distinct cards
• Open packs and chase escalating rarities
• Build squads around stats, roles, and chemistry
• Battle through unpredictable scenarios
• Grow your collection with daily rewards
• Track your record and compete through PvP systems
• Sync progress securely across devices

Every pull creates a new possibility. Every lineup tells a different story. Who makes your infinite squad?
